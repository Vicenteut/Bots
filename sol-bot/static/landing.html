What we publish

The Clam Letter covers geopolitics, macro events, and policy through a layered, contrarian editorial voice. Our content is distributed daily as text on Threads (@theclamletter) and as 9:16 vertical video reels on Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts.

How we publish

Our editorial workflow is supported by Sol Dashboard, an internal automation tool that:

  • Ingests breaking news from public RSS feeds and Telegram channels
  • Generates editorial copy in our editorial voice using LLMs
  • Renders short vertical videos with branded text overlays and music
  • Publishes to authorized social accounts via official platform APIs

All content is operator-reviewed before publication. No automated posting occurs without explicit human action.
